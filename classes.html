Timetable

DoBu Martial Arts provides a well-organised schedule to meet different needs. Classes for children are held from 3 to 5 PM, giving young martial artists a concentrated setting. Students can hone their abilities during open mat sessions, which are offered from 1 PM to 2:30 PM for those who want more practice. To provide individualised training, private tutoring is also available from 10:30 AM to 12 PM. In order to accommodate all skill levels and schedules, regular classes are held throughout the day to fill in the gaps between these particular sessions.
