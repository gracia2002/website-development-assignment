General Questions

Does joining require any prior experience?

A: Absolutely not! Our instructors customise lessons to each student's progress, and our beginner-friendly classes emphasise the essentials.

Q:What attire is appropriate for my first class?

A: Beginners can wear comfortable sportswear, such as shorts and a t-shirt. After registering, Judo/Karate uniforms (gis) are given out

Q:How does the class schedule work?

A:dynamic schedule that may be filtered by day or martial art is available on our Classes page. Every month, updates are published.

Q:Can I walk in without a reservation?

A: While drop-ins are acceptable for regular sessions, we advise using our portal to secure a space in advance, particularly for workshops.

Q: How do you handle cancellations?

A: Cancel at least 12 hours before class to avoid fees. The cost of late cancellations is Â£5.

Q: Are there age restrictions for classes?

A: We accept children ages 5 and up (Kids Jiu-Jitsu/Karate) and provide adult classes for teenagers and up.

Q: Do your instructors have first aid certification?

A: All coaches have first aid training, and our facility has an AED on site.

Q: Do you have lockers and showers?

A: Yes! All members have access to secure lockers, changing areas, and showers.
